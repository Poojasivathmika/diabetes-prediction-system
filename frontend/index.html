Diabetes Prevention Tips

1

Maintain Healthy Weight

Losing 5-10% of body weight can significantly reduce diabetes risk. Focus on sustainable lifestyle changes.

2

Stay Physically Active

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week. Regular exercise improves insulin sensitivity.

3

Eat Healthy Foods

Choose whole grains, fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins. Limit processed foods and sugary drinks.

4

Regular Health Checkups

Monitor blood sugar levels regularly and get comprehensive health screenings at least annually.

5

Manage Stress

Chronic stress can affect blood sugar levels. Practice meditation, yoga, or other relaxation techniques.

6

Get Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Poor sleep can increase insulin resistance.
